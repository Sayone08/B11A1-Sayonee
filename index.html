Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation sparks progress, creating new solutions and transforming ideas into practical reality.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures balance, preserving resources while supporting growth for future.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure connects communities, enabling progress through reliable systems and essential services.

Empowerment

Empowerment inspires individuals, building confidence and unlocking potential for lasting change.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a nation thriving on innovation and empowerment. Our dream is to build a sustainable, inclusive future for all. By focusing on education, green energy, and entrepreneurship, we unlock new potential. Together, we create a society without poverty or unemployment, driven by compassion. Technology will transform lives, ensuring progress reaches even the most marginalized. Collaboration across communities will make Bangladesh an example of resilience. Let's dream big, inspire action, and take bold steps toward this vision. The future depends on us; lets make Bangladesh 2.0 a reality today!"

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
